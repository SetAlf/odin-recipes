Pan Seared Garbanzo Bean and Egg Burrito

Home

A vegetarian burrito filled with scrambled eggs, pan seared chickepeas, sautéed bell peppers/onions, and cheese, all wrapped in a flour tortilla and seared shut with cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 flour tortilla, 10 inches
  • avocado oil spray or 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp thyme

Steps:

  1. Heat a medium non-stick frying pan to high heat and add strained and rinsed canned chickpeas, occasionally stirring until crisp and golden brown.
  2. Pour chickpeas into medium large bowl, lower the heat to medium, and spray the pan with avocado oil.
  3. Add three beaten eggs to pan and immediately begin stirring contents until scrambled.
  4. Add scrambled eggs to the medium large bowl with chickpeas.
  5. Add chopped bell peppers and onion to the pan, sautéing until tender and lightly browned.
  6. Pour the bell peppers and onions into the medium large bowl containing both the chickpeas and scrambled eggs.
  7. In the bowl, add 1/4 cup shredded cheese of your choice, garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, thyme, and mix the contents.
  8. In a pan on medium heat, add the flour tortilla and flip it, removing it when it is soft and warm.
  9. Put the tortilla on a plate, and pour the contents from the bowl onto the tortilla.
  10. Fold contents and tortilla into a burrito.
  11. In a pan on medium heat, sprinkle 1/4 cup shredded cheese in a line, and place bottom of burrito on the cheese.
  12. Flip the burrito to sear the top till brown once the cheese is crisp and golden
  13. Remove and plate burrito,letting cool for a couple minutes before eating.
  14. Enjoy!
