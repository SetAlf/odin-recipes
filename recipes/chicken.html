Pan Seared Chicken and Oven Baked Potatoes

Home

Pan seared chicken breasts served with cubed oven baked potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 russet potatoes
  • 4-5 tbsp olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • smoked paprika
  • black pepper
  • thyme

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. Rinse and cut two russet potatoes into one inch cubes
  2. Put cubed potatoes in a medium large bowl, adding garlic powder, smoked paprika, black pepper, thyme, and olive oil, tossing until evenly seasoned.
  3. Evenly spread out potatoes on a large tray covered with parchment paper and place into oven to let cook for thirty minutes or until golden brown.
  4. While the potatoes cook, slice the chicken breasts lengthwise into thin cutlets and season with garlic powder, black pepper, smoked paprika, thyme, and olive oil.
  5. On a non-stick frying pan set to medium high heat, spread olive oil on the pan before the placing chicken breast cutlets.
  6. Flip the cutlets after six minutes, and wait another six minutes before removing the cutlets to be plated with the cubed potatoes.
  7. Let cool for a couple minutes then enjoy!
